What It Is

The end-to-end home design platform nobody built yet.

An AI-powered platform built for your home. Whether moving into a new place or looking to renovate, it gives you the chance to see exactly what your space could look like before spending a single dollar.

You pick your style, your budget, your materials — and the platform handles everything else. Design, sourcing, delivery, and installation. All of it.

How It Works

01

Scan Your Space

A professional is sent out to scan your room using Canvas — a best-in-class 3D scanning tool — capturing exact dimensions with precision. No guesswork, no wrong-sized furniture showing up.

02

Pick Your Design

The AI platform generates 3 to 5 fully realized design options based on your style preferences and budget. Filter by material, price range, aesthetic. Fall in love with one. Lock it in.

03

One Day. Done.

Everything ships together to a local staging point and arrives in a single delivery. Vetted, background-checked installers show up and bring the whole room to life. One appointment. Zero chaos.

What Makes It Different

01

Canvas Partnership

Instead of relying on user-submitted measurements that are always slightly off, professional scanning techs use Canvas to capture precise 3D room data. The result is better data than any competitor has — and a design that actually fits.

02

Vetted Installer Network

A curated, rated installer network — hyper-specialized for furniture and home finishing. Every job comes with a satisfaction guarantee. If the finished room doesn't match what was approved on screen, someone comes back at no charge. No competitor offers this.

03

Wholesale Supplier Margins

Instead of just marking up retail prices, the platform partners directly with mid-size regional manufacturers — giving them a digital channel they'd never access otherwise. In exchange, wholesale pricing. Better margins, better value for customers.

04

Single Room Delivery

Every item for a given room ships together, staged at a local partner facility, and gets delivered and installed in one appointment. No waiting on six deliveries over three weeks. One truck, one day, done. Operationally hard. Massively differentiating.

05

Living Design Platform

After installation, the platform remembers every item in your home — dimensions, materials, colors, everything. It notifies you when something you own has a better version available, suggests seasonal refreshes, and gets smarter about your taste over time. A one-time transaction becomes a recurring relationship.

The Roadmap

MVP in 3 Phases

Prove the Design Experience

Before suppliers, installers, or logistics — show a customer a room and let them fall in love with a design. That's what makes them pull out a credit card. The first 5–10 orders get coordinated manually on purpose. You learn every pain point before automating anything.

  • Web app where customers upload photos or a Canvas scan
  • AI generates 3–5 design options based on style and budget
  • Itemized quote + consultation request flow
  • Stripe for payments from day one
  • Manual fulfillment coordination behind the scenes

Lock In Supply & Installation

Real customer orders create real leverage. Go to 3–5 regional furniture manufacturers and negotiate wholesale deals. Simultaneously recruit and vet the first 10–15 installers in one target city. Do everything in one market first — don't spread thin. This phase ends when a full order can be fulfilled without personal management at every step.

  • Wholesale pricing agreements with regional manufacturers
  • Vetted installer network in one metro area
  • Single-room delivery system — one truck, one day
  • Full order fulfillment without hand-holding

The Living Design Platform

Now the sticky layer gets added. Every completed customer gets a full home profile in the app. Personalized refresh suggestions, seasonal updates, upgrade notifications. This is where recurring revenue kicks in — and where it starts looking like a real company to investors.

  • Full home profile — every piece, material, and dimension stored
  • Upgrade and seasonal refresh push notifications
  • Taste profile that sharpens over time
  • Recurring revenue layer on top of one-time orders
  • Investor-ready story with compounding retention
